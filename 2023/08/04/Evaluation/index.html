How does this approach solve the problem?

This approach can solve most of problems of poor-quality education caused by teachers.

  1. Teachers who have received systematic training have more professional teaching skills and more modern teaching methods and can teach students more efficiently. In addition, teacher training has also strengthened their professionalism, and the quality of teachers has been greatly improved. All these laid the foundation for the development of quality education.

  2. Through the International Teacher Training Organization, teachers in vulnerable countries have access to educational resources from developed countries and are trained in the use of computers and the Internet. Through these resources, teachers can update their knowledge, strengthen their connection with the world, and prevent further marginalization of communities, providing a guarantee for the development of quality education.

  3. The establishment of the international teacher training organization enables more local people to see the prospect of teacher career development, and more people are willing to engage in the teacher profession, thus promoting the prosperity and development of the teacher profession and promoting the advancement of quality education.

What are the current challenges?

Lack of resources & poor school infrastructure

The education environment in fragile states is very difficult. Educational resources such as teaching materials and other teaching tools are very scarce, and purchasing and transporting from other countries will consume a lot of funds and manpower.

Lack of stable support

This is a charitable organization, and its manpower and funding sources are not stable, so we think it may be very difficult to sustain it in the long term.

What are the advantages of this approach over other solutions?

More professional

The quality of teachers is very important for quality education. To improve the efficiency and professionalism of teachers, there is no more professional and effective way than providing professional training to teachers. Teachers need to improve their teaching skills through learning.

More practical

The purpose of quality education is to make everyone not only receive basic education, but also access to a higher level of education. In the process of training, teachers in fragile countries not only learn how to teach students, but also strengthen their contact with the world through international teacher training organizations, which is a kind of broadening of their own vision. This is a higher level of education.

More impressive

If we want to attract more people to pay attention to the importance of the teaching industry to quality education, the impact of improving teachers’ salary and welfare as well as their social status will be short-lived. Only by fundamentally improving the quality of teachers can we achieve lasting impact
