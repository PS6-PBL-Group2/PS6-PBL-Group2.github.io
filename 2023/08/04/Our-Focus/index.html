# Teachers are the leading force of Quality Education

All of us understand the incredible importance of quality education in current society, which is the call of The Times and a key factor in promoting human progress. However, for people in fragile countries such as IGAD countries, access to quality education is often out of reach. In countries deeply affected by conflict and poverty, teachers play a central role in the peaceful and continued self-development of children. They are the implementers of education and the disseminators of knowledge; they are key to the success of any education system.

In our blog, we analyse the challenges faced by teachers in fragile countries in quality education in the problem section, and then show our solution and how it works in the solution section. Then we present our assessment of the solution and explain our reasons for choosing it in the evaluation section, combined with relevant success case in the example section.
